The growth of aircraft manufacturing, along with enhanced regulatory requirements over the last 20 years, have made it increasingly difficult for many peer organizations to obtain Supplemental Type Certifications (STCs) and Parts Manufacturing Approvals (PMAs).

Our design team has the extensive knowledge and experience regarding the certification of maintenance and avionics systems for both U.S. and E.U. Regulatory Agencies, as well as numerous Supplemental Type Certifications, internationally and domestically. We have also developed STCs for a number of companies, including Rockwell Collins, which have resulted in PMA’s. Because of this unique level of expertise, aircraft manufacturers and other design companies often contact our certified experts for special consultations.

Supplemental Type Certifications (STCs):

  • STC support for customers and other repair stations
  • STC development expertise for avionics systems

Current Maintenance STCs

  • STC Number: SA1450GL
    Description of the Type Design Change: Installation of electronic equipment racks in the aircraft cabin.
  • STC Number: SA1200GL
    Description of the Type Design Change: Limit maximum passenger seating capacity to nine or less.

  • FIS support

  • Structural
  • Flight test
