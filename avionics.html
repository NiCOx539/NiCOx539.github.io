C-Ray Avionics is home to a skilled avionics team that is constantly called upon for their industry-leading capabilities. With extensive experience in avionics system integration design, STC development, and service and maintenance on the most sophisticated avionics systems, the avionics capabilities offered by Pentastar are endless.

Avionics Design

The avionics systems designers at Pentastar are on the cutting edge, ensuring your aircraft is updated with the most innovative technology, from cockpit to cabin. Capabilities include Wi-Fi and high-speed data, entertainment systems, weather systems, cabin management systems, cabin lighting, and flight avionics.

Pentastar avionics systems design capabilities include: 

  • Wiring diagrams utilizing Auto CAD
  • Electronic systems integration
  • Flight manual supplements
  • Electrical load computation
  • Engineering orders
  • Design support for customers and repair stations
  • Instructions for continued airworthiness
  • In-house avionics design support
  • Onsite electrical DER

Structural design capabilities include:

  • Antenna placement
  • Radome integration design
  • Instrument panel design

Avionics Service & Maintenance

The avionics service technicians at C-Ray Avionics deliver an unmatched combination of quality, technology, superior customer service, and meticulous attention to detail. They maintain, service, upgrade and install some of the most complex and technologically advanced equipment on the market, and have capabilities on all size aircraft.

Pentastar holds a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) approved repair station certificate BTVR 626C and is a European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) approved repair station.

Avionics specific capabilities include:

  • Navigation
  • Radar
  • Electrical accessories/inverters
  • Batteries
  • 91.411 and 91.413
  • Pitot-static system
  • Communication
  • In-flight entertainment
  • Cabin management system
